Law Number 41

---------------------------------------------------

AVOID STEPPING INTO A GREAT MAN'S SHOES

What happensfirst always appears better and more original than what comes after.If you succeed a great man or have a famous parent, you will have to accomplish double their achievements to outshine them. Do not get lost in their shadow, or stuck in a past not of your own making: Establish your own name and identity by changing course. Slay the overbearing father, disparage his legacy, and gain power by shining in your own way.
