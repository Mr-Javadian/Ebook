Law Number 22

---------------------------------------------------

USE THE SURRENDER TACTIC: TRANSFORM WEAKNESS INTO POWER

When you are weaker, never fight for honor’s sake; choose surrender instead. Surrender gives you time to recover, time to torment and irritate your conqueror, time to wait for his power to wane. Do not give him the satisfaction offighting and defeating you —surrenderfirst. By turning the other cheek you infuriate and unsettle him. Make surrender a tool of power.
